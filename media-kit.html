Introduction

Hello, I'm Jenn, the voice behind Wanderlost + Found. I caught the travel bug on a summer-long backpacking trip through Europe during my university years and I've been on a mission to explore the world and get lost on my adventures ever since.

With a passion for photography and searching out unique experiences while exploring the globe, I created my brand to inspire others to be lost in new locations, find themselves in the moment, wander this Earth, and be astounded by it.
